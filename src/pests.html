Pest & Diseases

The use of AI technology to assist in the detection of plant and crop diseases is increasing in this modern age. The AI sensors can detect and decide which is the best action to take, such as recommending specific treatments or preventive measures based on the type of disease identified. Therefore, the accuracy of the training data is very crucial. The quality of the dataset that the crop detection system will use is essential. Misleading images can break the performance of the AI system.

For example, a bad AI dataset can lead to missing the early signs of mildew. The consequence of such errors can lead to a rapid spread of mildew and a reduction in yield.
A poor training dataset can cause unnecessary pesticide use due to the misidentification of beneficial insects.

All of this risk can be mitigated by ensuring various plant images for healthy and unhealthy plants are incorporated into the training dataset.
AI rich and diverse dataset is essential for consistency.
Regularly reviewing the AI dataset classification for accuracy is crucial.
