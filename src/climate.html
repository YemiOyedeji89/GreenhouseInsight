Climate

AI sensors in the greenhouse collect data to help control the climate, such as light levels, temperature, humidity, and CO₂ (carbon dioxide) concentration. These data are extracted and transformed to help people understand and make decisions about how to control the greenhouse's climate, which helps plants grow better and use resources more efficiently.

The following reading simulates the transformation and reporting of a good and a bad dataset to the dashboard for use. The bad data extracted was due to a faulty sensor with a 5% positive deviation from the good data. The inconsistency in the bad data will affect the accuracy of the dashboard reports.
