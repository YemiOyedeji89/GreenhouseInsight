Irrigation

There is a significant risk to the business if an error happens in the greenhouse sensors. Greenhouse AI automated systems rely heavily on the accuracy of sensor data to make real-time decisions about irrigation. This 5% error over a full year will impact the following:

Impact on the Greenhouse AI System

  • The AI system may provide a false optimal level for irrigation, which can result in high water wastage, as shown in the cumulative water wastage between July-September has about 40.9% water wastage.
  • It can lead to over-saturation of the root, which can affect the root's oxygen deprivation and cause fungal diseases.
  • Future prediction becomes inaccurate.
